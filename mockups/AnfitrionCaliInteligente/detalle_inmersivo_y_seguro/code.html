San Antonio Church

location_on Barrio San Antonio, Cali

A Colonial Gem

Perched atop the hill of San Antonio, this 18th-century chapel is the heart of Cali's oldest neighborhood. Its whitewashed walls and mud-tiled roof stand as a testament to the city's colonial past, offering the most iconic panoramic views of the "Sucursal del Cielo."
